About SJS Company

COMPANY HISTORY

Built on Dedication Since 2016

SJS Company was founded in 2016 by Samuel J. Sithole with a single truck and a vision to improve goods transportation in South Africa.

From humble beginnings in Gauteng, we expanded to Limpopo and Mpumalanga, building a reputation for speed, safety, and professionalism.

Today, we operate a fleet of over 50 vehicles, employ more than 200 professionals, and have successfully completed over 10,000 deliveries.

  • 2016 Founded in Johannesburg, Gauteng
  • 2018 Expanded to Limpopo & Mpumalanga
  • 2020 Fleet reached 25 vehicles
  • 2023 Launched real-time tracking system
  • 2025 50+ vehicles & 200 employees

Our Mission

To provide fast, reliable, and secure goods transportation that empowers businesses across South Africa to grow without limits.

Our Vision

To become South Africa's leading logistics partner — known for innovation, reliability, and excellence in every delivery.
